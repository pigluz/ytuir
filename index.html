In ytuir., you have to press correct keys quickly until the time runs out.

The tutorial is easy; you have 10 seconds and you will see 4 random symbols on the screen.
Those symbols are the equivalent of your keyboard keys, and here's the list of them:

↑ ─ W ← ─ A
↓ ─ S → ─ D
